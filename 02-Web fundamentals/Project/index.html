About
Background

I am a Cardiff based junior software developer passionate about writing clean and functional code. I have recently trained on a part-time software development course with _nology.

Experience

My first experience was whilst working as a community pharmacist. It was heavily admin-centered and I thought there must be a way to stream-line this whole process. I started dabbling with swift code making mobile apps and that led me into Codecademy where I learnt basics of HTML and CSS. From making apps, I was instantly fascinated by the problem solving concepts, so I decided that the next logical step would be to pursue it as a full-time career. This is where I began my journey at _nology and have not looked back since!
